# Product Vision

## Core Value Proposition
A technical, high-depth F1-style management simulation focused on real-time decision making and long-term team evolution.

## UX Principles
- **Telemetric Precision**: UI elements mimic real racing telemetry.
- **Micro-interactions**: Use of Svelte transitions for high-quality feel.
- **Responsive Logic**: Fully functional on desktop and tablet views.

## Functional Scope
- **Driver Lifecycle**: From Youth Academy scouting to free agency and retirement.
- **Financial Strategy**: Sponsor management vs. staff and facility upkeep.
- **Real-time Racing**: Active setup changes during practice and live race monitoring.

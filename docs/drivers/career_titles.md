# Driver Career Status Titles

This document details the career status titles used in **FTG Racing Manager 2026**. These titles are dynamically assigned to drivers based on their age, experience, statistics, and historical performance.

In the database, these titles are stored in their **English** version (mostly gender-neutral). Below are the descriptions and their equivalents for both masculine and feminine contexts.

---

## 1. The Olympus (El Olimpo)
Reserved for the absolute elite of the sport.

| Title (DB/EN) | Description | ES Masculino | ES Femenino |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Living Legend** | Multiple champion with nothing left to prove; their presence defines the era. | Leyenda Viva | Leyenda Viva |
| **Era Dominator** | A driver in their prime who makes the rest of the field look a class below. | Dominador de Época | Dominadora de Época |
| **The Heir / The Heiress** | The driver everyone knows will be champion as soon as they have the right car. | El Heredero | La Heredera |

---

## 2. The Old Guard (La Vieja Guardia)
Experienced drivers who provide stability and wisdom to the grid.

| Title (DB/EN) | Description | ES Masculino | ES Femenino |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Elite Veteran** | Many years at the top, consistent, but has passed their peak pure speed. | Veterano de Élite | Veterana de Élite |
| **Last Dance** | A driver clearly in their farewell season, whether official or not. | Último Baile | Último Baile |
| **Solid Specialist** | A grid staple for many years, guaranteed points, but likely never to be champion. | Especialista Consolidado | Especialista Consolidada |

---

## 3. New Blood (Sangre Nueva)
The potential future champions and breakout talents.

| Title (DB/EN) | Description | ES Masculino | ES Femenino |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Young Wonder** | A rookie or second-year driver breaking records for their age. | Joven Maravilla | Joven Maravilla |
| **Rising Star** | No longer a rookie, winning races and climbing the ranks quickly. | Estrella en Ascenso | Estrella en Ascenso |
| **Stuck Promise** | Someone who entered with high expectations but whose results have flattened. | Promesa Estancada | Promesa Estancada |

| Title (DB/EN) | Description | ES Masculino | ES Femenino |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Journeyman / Journeywoman** | Does the job, avoids errors, but rarely makes the headlines. | Cumplidor de Oficio | Cumplidora de Oficio |
